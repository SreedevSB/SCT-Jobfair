About

Career Guidance and Placement Unit, Sree Chitra Thirunal College of Engineering, Pappanamcode, Thiruvananthapuram is planning to organize a Job Fair at its college premises for the year 2019. This is the seventh time that our college would be organizing such a job fair, following the grand success of the first four job fairs that were conducted as part of our technical fest Cult a' Way 2012 and 2014, 2015 and 2016.


The Job Fair will bring together students from different colleges pursuing various engineering disciplines like Electronics and Communication, Mechanical, Electrical, Computer Science , Biotechnology and Biochemical Engineering as well as other non-engineering disciplines. We are expecting around 2000 students to participate in this mega event.
